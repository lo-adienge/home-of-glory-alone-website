Registrar's Office - Bureti Technical Training Institute
  • Registrar's Office - Bureti Technical Training Institute
Message

Bureti Technical Training institute is one of the institutions of higher learning in South Rift Region in Kenya. It is also one of the fastest growing institutions in the region. The institution has many on-going academic programs which have grown tremendously. We train and equip students with the technical and business skills as per the requirements of the labour market/industry. We admit students from all counties the country in different courses ranging from Artisan Courses, Craft Certificate Courses to Diploma Courses and we are now looking forward to start offering Higher Diploma for the approved courses very soon. Currently, the courses we offer are examined by Kenya National Examinations Council (KNEC) and KASNEB. As from 2022 the institution will also roll out Competency Based Education and Training (CBET). This curriculum will ensure that students are practically oriented which is in line with the National Government’s Big 4 Agenda. The Registrar’s Office is charged with the responsibility of admitting new students, registering all continuing students, updating student records when necessary, ensuring the integrity of student records, interpreting and enforcing academic policies and providing outstanding student services. The office also collaborates with the Academics office in curriculum implementation; collaborates with the Exams Office by ratifying examinations. Another responsibility is marketing the institute and its courses through local radio stations and through career talks in secondary schools, TVET sensitization programs among others. There is also organizing for participation in Trade Fairs and Exhibitions, coordinates Research and Innovation initiatives. Therefore, Registrar’s Office must complete these responsibilities while adapting to the ever-changing technologies and increasing service expectations from students, parents, and stakeholders in the public and private sectors. Therefore Bureti Technical Training Institute (BURETI) is the place to be in order to study a program of your choice so as to acquire knowledge and skills.

BURETI TTI is Center of Technology And Innovation.We are proud of being the Hub of technical education.
