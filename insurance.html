NOTICE TO STUDENTS: INSURANCE COVERS 2023

We are pleased to inform you that the students' insurance covers for the calendar year 2023 are now ready. This insurance policy will provide you with the necessary cover during your attachment and internship within the calendar year 2023. Please make sure to have a copy of your insurance cover with you at all times during your attachment or internship. You can now download your insurance policy by clicking on the link provided below and searching for your file using your admission number.
